Hotspot Search Task

You are about to participate in an experiment by the Harvard Neuromotor Lab. Your contribution to our research allows us to learn more about human decision-making patterns. Please read the following information carefully before proceeding.

Why we are doing this research:

We want to understand how people make trial & error decisions. What you will have to do: You will search for an invisible hotspot on the screen, guided by “hot” or “cold” feedback. Refusal to participate will involve no penalty.

Potential risks:

There are no risks anticipated in taking part in this study and you are free to leave at any time by clicking the “I’ve had enough! Pay me.” button located at the bottom right-hand corner of the browser window.

Privacy:

Your responses will be kept anonymous. We do not collect information that could be used to directly establish your identity. Duration: • 2 minutes for a short tutorial on the hotspot game and a small quiz to test knowledge of the rules. • 0 to 30 additional minutes for an extended bonus segment (pay by performance), following successful completion of tutorial and quiz.

Benefits:

There are no direct health-related or educational benefits for participating in this study.

Compensation:

• $0.15 for completing the initial tutorial and quiz. • $7.25/hour pay rate (approximate) for completing the extended bonus segment (about 30 minutes). This pay rate is made up of $0.01~0.05 for every additional hotspot game played during this segment (based on performance), and an additional “completion bonus” of up to $1.00 for completing the entirety of the experiment. • Payment will be administered through Amazon Mechanical Turk’s payment system.
